My Corona diary

Coping with the whole idea of a lockdown hasn't been easy. Boredom was at its peak so I had to find new activities to occupy myself with. The following some of the activities I occupied myself with.

  1. Listening to Podcasts

    Listening to Podcasts became a new thing I couldn't do away with during the lockdown. It replaced radio for me. It was very informative, entertaining and refreshing since it wasnt like the normal radio programs i was used to . I really hope to continue with it now that the lockdown is over but we are still observing the precautionary guideline available. These are some of my favorite podcasts channel and I hope you would try them out.

    • Con Artists
    • Quarantine Diaries
    • Ask a doctor

  2. Learning how to Cook

    Before the corona lockdown days I wouldn't have accepted any cooking challenge with anyone, so this whole lockdown period served me with the oppurtunity to learn how to cook. I can't call myself a world class chef now but I can cook indomie and rice with some flex on you. The point is I learnt how to cook one or two simple dishes that might be handy in the coming days.
